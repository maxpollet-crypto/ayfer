About Ayfer

I know what it feels like to lose yourself.


I was born in southern Turkey, raised in Istanbul and have been living in San Francisco for the past 18 years. My journey across cultures, life experiences and deep inner work has profoundly shaped the woman I am today and the work I now share with others.

For much of my life, I searched for love, belonging, certainty and answers outside of myself. On the surface, I often looked strong and capable, but internally I was carrying emotional pain, uncertainty and patterns I didn’t yet know how to break.

My spiritual awakening began over a decade ago through a deeply painful heartbreak… a turning point that led me into my own hero’s journey of healing, self-discovery and transformation.

What initially felt like loss became the beginning of a profound awakening. It led me inward into healing, self-inquiry, emotional growth, embodiment, yoga, spirituality, and a deeper relationship with myself.

Along this path, I also became a certified yoga teacher and began facilitating soul sister circles and community gatherings… spaces where women could slow down, reconnect and remember who they are.

I know what it feels like to feel disconnected from yourself while silently carrying the exhaustion of wanting your life to change, but not knowing how to create that change.

Over the years, I began releasing old patterns, rebuilding self-trust and reconnecting with who I truly was beneath the conditioning, fear and self-doubt.

Through years of inner work, self-discovery, yoga, and devotion to my soul’s purpose, I realized that my deepest calling is helping women heal, reconnect with themselves, and step into the woman they are becoming.

I didn’t plan to become a Spiritual Life Coach. Life led me here.

Today, through The Becoming Method, I support women on their own journey of healing and transformation… helping them come back to themselves, release old patterns and rise into the woman they’re becoming.

Readiness

This work is for you
and it’s not for everyone.

This work is for you if…

  • You feel disconnected from yourself and long to come back home to who you truly are.
  • You keep repeating emotional or relationship patterns you’re finally ready to understand and break.
  • You’re exhausted by overthinking, self-doubt, or carrying emotional weight on your own.
  • You crave deeper clarity, inner alignment, and a more grounded relationship with yourself.
  • You’re ready for meaningful transformation through honest inner work, not temporary inspiration.

This may not be for you if…

  • You’re looking for quick fixes rather than the deeper work real transformation requires.
  • You want someone to tell you what to do instead of helping you find your own answers.
  • You’re not ready to honestly face the emotional patterns that continue keeping you stuck.
  • You’re seeking surface motivation rather than deeper healing, growth, and meaningful change.
  • You’re unwilling to make the space honest transformation asks of your time and energy.
Transformation begins when you’re ready to meet yourself honestly.
